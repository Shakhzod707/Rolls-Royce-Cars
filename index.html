Rolls-Royce Ghost

The Rolls-Royce Ghost is a full-sized luxury car manufactured by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars.
The "Ghost" nameplate, named in honour of the Silver Ghost, a car first produced in 1906,
was announced in April 2009 at the Auto Shanghai show.
The production model was officially unveiled at the 2009 Frankfurt Motor Show.

About Us

Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited was created as a wholly owned subsidiary of BMW in 1998
after BMW licensed the rights to the Rolls-Royce brand name and logo from Rolls-Royce Holdings plc
and acquired the rights to the Spirit of Ecstasy and Rolls-Royce grille shape trademarks from Volkswagen AG.
Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited has been manufacturing Rolls-Royce branded cars since 2003.
